Being the oldest institution of higher learning in the United States, Harvard University is a private Ivy League research university that was established in 1636 in Cambridge, Massachusetts. It is one of the most prestigious universities in the US Education System and the world having the highest university endowment.Having the largest academic library in the world, the university is devoted to excellence in teaching, learning, and research. The school has two main academic facets which are the Harvard College (liberal arts and sciences college for undergraduates) and Harvard University for graduate students.
Over 36,000 students are enrolled in 12 different schools offering some unparalleled programs to its candidates. With an international population of nearly 13% and a student-faculty ratio of 7:1, the institute is indeed a site of learning and growth that promotes diversity and inclusion among its students.Its campus houses ten hospitals, five museums, two theatres, three athletic facilities, 28 libraries, 12 residential buildings, several academic and administrative buildings and other buildings.A typical residence hall has a bed, desk and chair, desk lamp, bookcase, bureau, and closet or wardrobe, Wi-Fi, laundry service, common areas, kitchens with a microwave, stove, oven, kettle, and refrigerator, vending machines, each hall with Cable and DVD set, washers, dryers, and a computer lab.
